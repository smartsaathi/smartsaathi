Iqama Expire Ho Gaya? Ghabraye Nahi — Yeh Karein (2026 Guide)

Saudi Arabia mein kaam karne wale Pakistani aur Bangladeshi workers ke liye — seedhi, saaf, Urdu mein guide.

Pehle Yeh Samjhein — Ghabrane Ki Zaroorat Nahi

Bohat se bhai sunte hain ke "Iqama expire ho gaya to seedha deportation ho jata hai" — yeh sach nahi hai.

Iqama expire hona aur "huroob" (absconding) status — yeh do alag cheezein hain. Sirf Iqama ki tareekh nikal jaane se aapko khud-ba-khud huroob nahi lagaya jata. Huroob sirf tab lagta hai jab kafeel/employer khud officially report karta hai ke worker kaam chhod gaya hai. Agar aap kaam par ja rahe hain, sab normal hai, sirf renewal late ho gaya hai — to yeh sirf ek fine ka maamla hai, deportation ka nahi.

Lekin late karna bhi sahi nahi — jaldi sambhal lena hi behtar hai. Neeche poora tarika hai.

Iqama Expire Hone Par Kya Hota Hai

Jab Iqama ki tareekh nikal jaati hai, yeh cheezein ruk jaati hain:

  • 🏦 Bank account se paisa nikalna ya bhejna mushkil ho sakta hai
  • 📱 Mobile SIM band ho sakti hai
  • 🏠 Naya rent agreement nahi ban sakta
  • 🖥️ Absher aur dusri government services band ho jaati hain
  • 🚓 Police checking mein masla ho sakta hai

Fine kitna lagta hai (Saudi Arabia, 2026):
Pehli baar late hone par — SAR 500 ka fine.
Dusri baar — fine aur badh jaata hai.
Lagataar kayi mahine tak renewal na hone par — huroob ka khatra badh jaata hai.

Sabse Pehla Kaam — Apna Status Check Karo

Ghabra kar kuch karne se pehle, pehle yeh dekho ke maamla kitna serious hai.

Tarika 1 — Absher App Se (Sabse Aasan)

  1. Absher app kholo (ya absher.sa website)
  2. Apne account mein login karo
  3. "Resident ID Expiry" ya "Iqama Status" inquiry par jao
  4. Apna Iqama number daalo
  5. Status aur exact expiry date dikh jayegi

Tarika 2 — MHRSD Website Se (Account Ki Zaroorat Nahi)

  1. mhrsd.gov.sa website par jao
  2. Language: Urdu ya English select karo
  3. Iqama number aur date of birth daalo
  4. Captcha bharo → Next
  5. Status turant dikh jayega

Yeh dono tarike free hain aur Iqama number ke saath 2 minute mein ho jaate hain.

Ab Karna Kya Hai — Step by Step

Agar Aap Company Mein Kaam Karte Hain (Sponsored Employee)

Saudi rule yeh hai ke Iqama renew karna employer/kafeel ki zimmedari hoti hai — paisa bhi employer deta hai, aap se nahi kaata ja sakta.

Aap yeh karein:

  1. HR/Kafeel ko foran batayein — likhit mein (WhatsApp message bhi theek hai, proof ke liye)
  2. Pooch lein: "Mera Iqama renewal kab ho raha hai?"
  3. Agar HR taal-mataal kare, to Qiwa platform ka zikar karein — wahan se employer renewal start kar sakta hai, chahe aap Saudi ke bahar hi ho
  4. Apna passport valid hai ya nahi — confirm kar lein (Iqama renewal ke liye passport ki kam az kam 6 mahine validity chahiye)
  5. Traffic fines (agar koi hai) — pehle clear karein, warna renewal rukk jata hai

Sample WhatsApp message employer/HR ko bhejne ke liye:

"Assalamu Alaikum. Mera Iqama [date] ko expire ho raha hai/ho gaya hai. Please renewal process shuru karein. Agar koi document ya information chahiye to bata dein. Shukriya."

Agar Employer Renewal Nahi Kar Raha

Yeh sabse zyada pareshan karne wali situation hai — lekin aapke paas raasta hai.

  1. Labour Office (MHRSD) ki helpline par call karein: 19911
  2. Apni shikayat darj karayein — ke employer Iqama renew nahi kar raha
  3. Agar 3 mahine tak salary na mile, contract khatam ho gaya ho, ya employer ne labor law tod di ho — to aap bina employer ki ijazat ke bhi Qiwa platform se transfer apply kar sakte hain
  4. Pakistani workers — Riyadh mein Pakistan Embassy se bhi raabta karein: 011-4880840
  5. Bangladeshi workers — Bangladesh Embassy: 011-2823141

Yaad Rakhne Wali Baatein

  • Iqama expire hona = deportation nahi, sirf fine ka khatra
  • Renewal karwana employer ki zimmedari hai, paisa bhi unka
  • Apna status Absher ya MHRSD website par 2 minute mein check karein
  • Employer taal-mataal kare to Labour Office helpline 19911 par call karein
